# Fundraising Performance & Data Quality Analysis

## Overview

This project evaluates fundraising campaign performance using supporter, donation and campaign datasets.

The analysis combines data quality auditing, validation, cleansing and business intelligence techniques to assess campaign effectiveness, donor behaviour, return on investment (ROI), communication channel performance and fundraising opportunities.

Before analysis, a structured data quality review was conducted to identify and resolve issues relating to completeness, consistency, accuracy and integrity across multiple datasets.

The objective was to produce reliable, decision-ready insights to support fundraising strategy, donor engagement and operational improvement.

---

## Business Questions

This analysis was designed to answer the following questions:

- Which campaigns generated the highest fundraising income?
- Which campaigns delivered the strongest return on investment?
- Which donor segments contributed the greatest value?
- Which communication channels performed most effectively?
- Are there demographic patterns in donor behaviour?
- Are there geographic differences in fundraising performance?
- What data quality issues could impact reporting accuracy?
- What actions could improve future fundraising outcomes?

---

## Data Quality & Validation

A structured audit process was performed before analysis to improve reliability and trust in reporting outputs.

### Data Quality Activities

- Duplicate supporter detection and investigation
- Email standardisation and validation
- Phone number standardisation and error detection
- Date format standardisation
- Missing value assessment
- Campaign naming standardisation
- Channel label standardisation
- Communication preference checks
- Join validation across supporter, campaign and response datasets
- Outlier and anomaly identification

### Key Issues Identified

- Missing email addresses
- Missing dates of birth
- Missing postcode information
- Duplicate supporter profiles
- Invalid phone numbers
- Future-dated records requiring review
- Missing supporter identifiers
- Communication preference inconsistencies
- Donation outliers requiring validation

All identified issues were documented within an audit log together with remediation actions and review outcomes.

## Key Findings

### Campaign Performance

Winter Appeal 2024 generated the highest fundraising income (£109,036) from 119 responses, while Volunteer Thank You Dinner delivered the strongest return on investment (6.1x).

Campaign analysis demonstrated that the highest-income campaigns were not always the most efficient campaigns.

### Donor Segmentation

Major Donors contributed the largest share of fundraising income (£288,703), significantly exceeding all other supporter segments.

This highlights the importance of donor retention and relationship management.

### Channel Effectiveness

Email and Direct Mail generated the highest fundraising income, while Digital campaigns demonstrated strong fundraising potential and delivered higher average gift values than several traditional channels.

### Demographic Insights

Supporters aged 50 and above generated the majority of fundraising income, particularly Major Donors and Legacy Pledgers responding through Email and Direct Mail campaigns.

This represents a significant fundraising strength but also highlights a long-term sustainability risk if younger supporter engagement is not improved.

### Geographic Performance

Income was concentrated across several high-performing regions, including South Glamorgan, Hampshire and Oxfordshire.

---

## Strategic Insights

### Revenue Concentration Risk

Fundraising income is heavily concentrated among Major Donors and Legacy Pledgers. While this supports strong short-term performance, it increases dependence on a relatively small supporter base.

### Demographic Sustainability Risk

Supporters aged 50 and above generated the majority of fundraising income. Future fundraising performance may depend on improving engagement among younger supporter groups.

### Efficiency Versus Income

Winter Appeal 2024 generated the highest income, while Volunteer Thank You Dinner delivered the highest ROI. This demonstrates the importance of evaluating both revenue generation and campaign efficiency when allocating future budgets.

---

## Recommendations

Based on this analysis, five actions could strengthen future fundraising performance:

1. Prioritise campaigns that deliver strong return on investment.
2. Review high-cost fundraising events such as the Summer Gala.
3. Focus future campaigns on high-performing channels including Email and Direct Mail.
4. Strengthen relationships with Major Donors and Legacy Pledgers.
5. Develop engagement strategies to attract younger supporters.

These recommendations focus on improving fundraising efficiency while supporting long-term donor sustainability.
